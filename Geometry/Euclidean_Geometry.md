# Euclidean Geometry

`Conversions`

1 degree = 60 minutes <br>
1 radian = (pi/180) * degree <br>

`Arc & Chord on a Circle`

**Arc**   = r × θ          (θ must be in radians)<br>
**Chord** = 2 × r × sin(θ/2)

`Soddy Circles`

**Curvature** of a circle = `k = 1 / r`<br>
